Privacy Policy

The "Manage Accounts Facebook" extension is committed to protecting your privacy. We strictly do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data from our users at any time.

All functionalities of this extension are designed to operate solely on your local device. This means that any cookies, session data, or user information processed by the extension remain on your machine. No data is ever sent to external servers or accessible by us or any third parties.

Rest assured, your privacy and data security are our top priority. Since no data is collected, shared, or stored, your personal information stays entirely within your control on your local device.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
